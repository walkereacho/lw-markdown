# Ordered Lists

A simple numbered list:

1. First step
2. Second step
3. Third step

A list starting from a different number:

5. Fifth item
6. Sixth item
7. Seventh item

Nested ordered list:

1. First main item
   1. Sub-item one
   2. Sub-item two
      1. Deep sub-item
      2. Another deep item
   3. Sub-item three
2. Second main item
3. Third main item
